Green Economic Zone

My role as the solo developer on the Green Economic Zone (GEZ) project was an exhilarating journey into the world of web development. Tasked with the challenge of converting a WordPress-based website into a meticulously crafted, hard-coded multi-page platform, I set out to create an online experience that would not only be visually striking but also highly user-friendly.

One of the project's standout features was its heavy reliance on images. Optimizing the display of these images while maintaining exceptional UI and UX was a paramount objective. I understood that the visual appeal and performance of the website were critical in conveying the essence of the Green Economic Zone.

To achieve this, I harnessed the power of React, a versatile JavaScript library known for its flexibility and performance capabilities. Pairing React with the streamlined styling of Tailwind CSS, I was able to breathe new life into the website's design. The result was not just a transformation; it was a metamorphosis.

My role extended beyond coding; it encompassed problem-solving, creativity, and an unwavering commitment to delivering a polished, image-rich website that exceeded expectations.
